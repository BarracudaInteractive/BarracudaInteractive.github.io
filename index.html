Rally Team Tactics (RTT)

Our first game has finally crossed the finish line! RTT represents our initial venture into the industry, and we poured our utmost effort and passion into its creation. If you're curious to learn more about the game, allow us to provide you with a glimpse of the underlying concept:

RTT deviates from the conventional racing game format. In this unique experience, you assume the role of a former pilot who, unfortunately, lost his right leg in a rally race accident. Unable to participate in races any longer, he chooses to become a mentor for aspiring rally racers.

Rather than directly controlling the vehicle, your responsibility lies in analyzing the track and offering instructions as a co-pilot. In this turn-based racing game, trial and error play a crucial role, as discovering the optimal strategy may require several attempts. Balancing the vehicle on the road while monitoring damage and fuel levels may seem challenging, but RTT provides players with the necessary guidance to comprehend the systems and ultimately master them, aiming to achieve the best times on the leaderboards.

Eager to know more? Curious for more details? Stay tuned to our social media channels for updates on the game's post-launch content and community support!

About Us
