Rally Team Tactics (RTT)

Our first (and only, for now) project, Rally Team Tactics, which we will call RTT from now on.

This game combines racing games with puzzles, not belonging to both genres 100%. In this game you play a former rally driver who, unfortunately, had a fatal accident. He managed to survive, but lost both legs. Now unable to drive again, they must train the new generation of rally drivers.

In gameplay it will consist of giving instructions to the new drivers so that they complete the circuits in the most efficient way possible, without spending a lot of gasoline, and without exploding the car if possible.
